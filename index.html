Looking to have fun learning the perodic table?!

Well, this site can help you! Dedicated to middle schoolers and high schoolers, (and made by a middle schooler) this website will help you learn the periodic table in a fun way! What are you waiting for? Exit out of the pop out, and start. (You start by clicking the START button, but I think you know that already)

Periodic Table Game Rules
Alright! First you click the "start" button. [Look above]

Then, you type in your answer![Look above]




And, of course. You submit it, clicking the "submit button".[Look above]



After that, you will get three alerts. If it is GREEN, that means it is correct! If it's red, that means it is incorrect, and if it is yellow - well you forgot to answer the question![Look above]

Now your all set! Have fun

FYI: You lose your progress when you RELOAD or EXIT the page.
Please note that you will not be able to click out of this modal until you answer ALL the questions, otherwise, your progress will be lost.
